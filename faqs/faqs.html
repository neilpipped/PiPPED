What are you providing?

If you're an entrepreneur or micro business in the infancy of your journey looking to commence your online presence, we'd like to have a crack at joining you in building your website and LinkedIn profile.

Why is online presence even important?

During the early stages of your business, you may not feel the need for a digital footprint. However, as you scale up, you may feel your competitors take up market share, so it is imperative that your customers know who you are, from at least a static website (like this!) and LinkedIn profile with basic contact info.

You don't have practical experience. Why should I partner with you?

For our lack of experience, we offer our services pro bono. What we lack in experience though, we make up for in enthusiasm and willingness to work with you until we reach a suitable outcome. Our own LinkedIn profiles have been active for many years, amassing networks of thousands of connections around the world - we will use this experience to assist you grow your brand.

If I don't pay anything, what's the catch?

If you're satisfied with our services, we only ask for word-of-mouth to your network and positive reviews on your socials.

I'm not in Perth, or even Australia. Are you still able to assist me?

Absolutely. With the invention of laptop computers, remote work and partnerships have actually been possible for at least the last two decades. It took a global pandemic in 2019 for most people to realise this but you don't actually need to meet in-person to do business. With a combination of resources such as instant messaging, e-mail and the traditional telephone, communication is as easy as ever whether you're in the same city or on the other side of the world.
